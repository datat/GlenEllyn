In a few cities, some believers have shed man-made affiliations and returned to the original ground of oneness.

To leave every other separating circle and gather in one fellowship solely with others God placed together in our city is to choose the church of God's choice. (Just as in a family, we don't choose our own brothers and sisters!) Even if just a few return to this original ground of meeting, we believe God will have a way to work out what He desires in a city for His testimony.
